MIS Day Season 1

East West University hosted MIS day for the second time on the 24th of July; an all day long event with the active participation of students from the courses MIS 101 and MIS 305 of the current semester. All the participating students for this event were recruited and trained under the supervision and guidance of their faculty Bushra Humyra Esha. The event was bustling with fun activities with the collaboration of all the students involved; with an open invitation to the entire university.

MIS Day Season2

The event was brought into full gear with the presence of the honorable guest Masum Khan, Country Business Manager-Nestle Professional from Nestle Bangladesh Limited. The crowd was motivated by his enlightening speech on the contribution of AI (artificial intelligence) in the future of IT sector in digital Bangladesh, giving impactful insights on real-time projects and helping the students get a glimpse of corporate world interactions and future propositions of IT.

MIS Day Season3

The event was also packed with out of the box creative activities, starting from debates, drama performances, and powerful presentations, all on various fields relating to IT development; emphasizing about the future potentials, challenges and ongoing ventures of different startups in Bangladesh and all over the world. The current hot and happening applications like Uber, Pathao, and Nogor were introduced to the crowd, as well as real word problems like “People get trapped into cybercrimes via social media” was orchestrated into a drama act by the students. Students also talked about Big Data, Deep Web, Next Generation Mobile Phones, Brain fingerprinting, Moore’s Law, Internet Banking etc. All in all, the teamwork of all the participants didn’t leave any room empty, filling up the event with sensational exhibitions all day long for their audience.

MIS Day Season 4

This was an exciting initiative taken at East West University to get the students to come out of the plain words in a text book into a fun platform for a day, where everyone from various majors and departments got together to learn and share different ideas on management information, IT and other ventures; exhibiting an exciting, fun and diverse side of the subject compared to their text books.
